<p><p>The irrigation automation market is divided into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic systems. Fully automatic systems operate independently, using advanced sensors and controllers to monitor soil moisture levels, weather conditions, and crop needs, ensuring optimal water distribution without human intervention. In contrast, semi-automatic systems require some manual input or adjustments, allowing users to set schedules or activate watering based on specific criteria, providing a balance between automation and user control for efficiency in water management.</p></p>
