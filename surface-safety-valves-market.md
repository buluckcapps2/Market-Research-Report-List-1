<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Safety Valve</li><li>Pneumatic Safety Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Safety Valves market comprises two main types: Hydraulic Safety Valves and Pneumatic Safety Valves. Hydraulic Safety Valves utilize hydraulic pressure to automatically shut off flow during abnormal conditions, ensuring safety in oil and gas operations. In contrast, Pneumatic Safety Valves use air pressure to activate and control the valve’s operation, ideal for environments requiring faster response times. Both types are crucial for preventing accidents and maintaining safe operations in various industries, particularly in oilfield applications.</p></p>
