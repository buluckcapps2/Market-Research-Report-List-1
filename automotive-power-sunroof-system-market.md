<p><p>The automotive power sunroof system market features three primary types: built-in sunroofs, sliding sunroofs, and panoramic sunroofs. Built-in sunroofs are integrated into the car's roof structure, offering fixed glass panels for light without ventilation. Sliding sunroofs can be opened and closed by sliding the panel back, allowing for natural airflow. Panoramic sunroofs provide an expansive glass roof that covers most of the vehicle's top, enhancing the spaciousness and providing a unique aesthetic appeal while allowing plenty of light into the cabin.</p></p>
