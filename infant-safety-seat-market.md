<p><ul><li>Rearward-facing baby seat</li><li>Combination seat (Rearward and Forward-facing)</li><li>Forward-facing child seat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infant safety seat market features three primary types. Rearward-facing baby seats are designed for infants and young toddlers, providing optimal head and neck support during collisions. Combination seats serve both rearward-facing for younger children and can be switched to forward-facing as they grow. Forward-facing child seats cater to older toddlers and preschoolers, utilizing a harness system for secure positioning. Each type emphasizes safety and adaptability to meet the changing needs of children as they develop.</p></p>
