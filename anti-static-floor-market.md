<p><ul><li>Direct Laying Anti-Static Floor</li><li>Anti-Static Access Floor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-static floor market encompasses two main types: direct laying anti-static floors and anti-static access floors. Direct laying anti-static floors are installed directly on the subfloor, providing a seamless surface that minimizes dust and static buildup. In contrast, anti-static access floors feature elevated tiles that allow for easy access to electrical wiring and HVAC systems, while also reducing static electricity. Both types are crucial in environments like data centers and electronics manufacturing, where static control is essential for equipment protection.</p></p>
