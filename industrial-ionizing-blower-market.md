<p><ul><li>Horizontal Ionizing Blower</li><li>Suspension Ionizing Blower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial ionizing blower market is categorized mainly into two types: horizontal ionizing blowers and suspension ionizing blowers. Horizontal ionizing blowers are designed to direct ionized air horizontally across surfaces, effectively neutralizing static electricity in various applications. In contrast, suspension ionizing blowers are typically used in environments where products or components are suspended, providing ionized air from above to stabilize charged particles without direct contact. Both types enhance productivity by reducing static and improving product integrity in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
