<p><p>The Cloud Videoconferencing Solution market is segmented into two main types: Scalable Video Coding (SVC) and Advanced Video Coding (AVC). SVC allows for flexible video streaming by enabling the adaptation of video quality based on network conditions, making it ideal for varying bandwidth situations. AVC, on the other hand, provides high-quality video compression but lacks the adaptability of SVC. Both types cater to diverse user needs, enhancing the overall videoconferencing experience by optimizing performance and resource utilization.</p></p>
