<p><p>The industrial centrifugal separator market is primarily divided into two types: liquid-liquid separation and liquid-solid separation. Liquid-liquid separation focuses on separating two immiscible liquids, often used in oil-water separation or wastewater treatment processes. In contrast, liquid-solid separation is designed to separate solids from a liquid medium, commonly utilized in mining, food processing, and chemical industries. Both types leverage centrifugal force to enhance efficiency, improve purity, and optimize processes across various applications.</p></p>
