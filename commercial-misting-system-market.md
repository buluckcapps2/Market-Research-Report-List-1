<p><ul><li>Low Pressure Misting System</li><li>Medium Pressure Misting System</li><li>High Pressure Misting System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial misting system market includes three main types: low pressure, medium pressure, and high pressure. Low pressure systems operate typically at 10-100 psi, providing a cost-effective solution for enhancing humidity and cooling. Medium pressure systems function at 100-500 psi, offering improved mist dispersion for larger areas. High pressure systems, exceeding 1000 psi, create ultra-fine mist droplets that evaporate quickly, maximizing cooling efficiency and minimizing water usage. Each type serves specific applications based on cooling needs and environmental settings.</p></p>
