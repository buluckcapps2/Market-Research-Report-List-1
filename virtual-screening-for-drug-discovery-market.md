<p><strong>The Virtual Screening for Drug Discovery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structure-based Virtual Screening (SBVS)</li><li>Ligand-based Virtual Screening (LBVS)</li><li>Hybrid Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Virtual screening for drug discovery involves computational techniques to identify potential drug candidates from large compound libraries. Structure-based virtual screening (SBVS) focuses on the 3D structures of target proteins, utilizing molecular docking to evaluate interactions with potential ligands. Ligand-based virtual screening (LBVS), on the other hand, relies on known active compounds to predict the activity of new ones by analyzing chemical similarities. Hybrid methods combine both SBVS and LBVS approaches to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of the screening process.</p></p>
