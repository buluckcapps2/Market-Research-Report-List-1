<p><p>The medium power transformer market, covering voltage ratings from 5 kV to 35 kV, includes two primary types: dry-type transformers and oil-immersed transformers. Dry-type transformers use air for cooling, making them safer and suitable for indoor or confined spaces, while oil-immersed transformers utilize oil for insulation and cooling, offering higher efficiency and capacity for outdoor applications. The choice between these types often depends on installation requirements, environmental considerations, and specific application needs in industrial and commercial sectors.</p></p>
