<p><ul><li>Two-pole Motor</li><li>Four-pole Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power seat adjustment motor market primarily includes two types of motors: two-pole and four-pole motors. Two-pole motors are simpler and typically used in basic seat adjustment functions, offering adequate torque for lightweight applications. In contrast, four-pole motors provide higher torque and smoother operation, making them suitable for more advanced seat adjustments, including memory settings and multiple positioning options. The choice between these motor types depends on the complexity of vehicle seat features and performance requirements.</p></p>
