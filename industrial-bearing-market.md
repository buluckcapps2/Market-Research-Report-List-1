<p><ul><li>Spherical roller bearings (SRB)</li><li>Deep Groove Ball Bearing (DGBB)</li><li>Cylindrical roller bearings (CRB)</li><li>Double-row Tapered Roller Bearing (DTRB)</li><li>Spherical Thrust Roller Bearing (STRB)</li><li>Angular contact ball bearings (ACBB)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial bearing market comprises various types designed for specific applications. Spherical roller bearings (SRB) accommodate heavy loads and shaft misalignment. Deep groove ball bearings (DGBB) offer versatility with low friction for high-speed operations. Cylindrical roller bearings (CRB) handle radial loads effectively. Double-row tapered roller bearings (DTRB) support both radial and axial loads. Spherical thrust roller bearings (STRB) manage axial loads, while angular contact ball bearings (ACBB) excel in high-speed, high-load applications. Other bearings cater to niche requirements.</p></p>
