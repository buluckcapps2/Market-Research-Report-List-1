<p><strong>The Prepainted Steel Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE Prepainted</li><li>HDP Prepainted</li><li>SMP Prepainted</li><li>PVDF Prepainted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prepainted steel coil market features several types based on the coating used. PE (Polyester) prepainted coils offer good weather resistance and color retention, suitable for indoor and low-exposure outdoor applications. HDP (High-Durability Polyester) prepainted coils provide enhanced durability and resistance, making them ideal for harsher environments. SMP (Silicone Modified Polyester) prepainted coils have improved flexibility and UV resistance. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) prepainted coils deliver exceptional weathering performance and color stability, making them suitable for demanding architectural applications.</p></p>
