<p><ul><li>Cassette Type</li><li>Bte Type</li><li>Ear Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Hearing Aids market features three primary types: Cassette Type, BTE (Behind-The-Ear) Type, and Ear Type. Cassette Type hearing aids are compact and versatile, allowing users to switch between different sound programs easily. BTE Type models are worn behind the ear, offering a blend of power and comfort, suitable for various hearing losses. Ear Type hearing aids, often termed in-the-ear (ITE), fit directly within the concha, providing a discreet option that is popular among users seeking aesthetic appeal and convenience.</p></p>
