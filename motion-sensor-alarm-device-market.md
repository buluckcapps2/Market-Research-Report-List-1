<p><ul><li>Passive Infrared (PIR) Type</li><li>MircoWave (MW) Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motion sensor alarm device market is categorized into several types. Passive Infrared (PIR) sensors detect body heat and are widely used for security applications due to their effectiveness in recognizing movement. Microwave (MW) sensors emit microwave signals and can cover larger areas, detecting motion through walls and objects. Other types include dual technology sensors that combine PIR and MW for enhanced accuracy, as well as ultrasonic sensors that use sound waves. Each type serves specific needs in both residential and commercial security systems.</p></p>
