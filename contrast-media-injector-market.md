<p><p>The contrast media injector market is categorized into three main types: single-head, dual-head, and triple-head injectors. Single-head injectors feature one injection site, suitable for basic procedures. Dual-head injectors allow simultaneous administration of different contrast agents, enhancing efficiency and versatility during imaging. Triple-head injectors offer even more functionality, enabling the use of multiple agents and improved workflow in complex imaging scenarios. Each type caters to varying clinical needs, influencing the choice based on procedure complexity and required efficiency.</p></p>
