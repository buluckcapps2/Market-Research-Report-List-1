<p><p>The robotic sandblasting systems market encompasses several types: Gantry Type, Wall-mounted, and Adsorption Type. Wall-mounted systems are compact and efficient, ideal for limited spaces, allowing precision cleaning or surface preparation. Adsorption-type systems utilize suction mechanisms to extract particles, enhancing control and minimizing waste.
