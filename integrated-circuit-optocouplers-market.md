<p><p>The Integrated Circuit Optocouplers market comprises various types based on their performance and application suitability. Phototransistor optocouplers utilize a phototransistor to provide electrical isolation and signal transfer, ideal for low-frequency applications. Photoelectric Darlington optocouplers enhance sensitivity and gain, suitable for high-reliability tasks. Photoelectric Thyristor optocouplers enable high-voltage switching, making them ideal for power control applications. Photoelectric Triac optocouplers facilitate AC load control, providing a robust solution for interfacing and managing electrical systems efficiently. Each type serves distinct needs within the market.</p></p>
