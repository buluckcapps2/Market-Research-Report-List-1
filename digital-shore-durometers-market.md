<p><p>Digital Shore Durometers measure the hardness of various materials using different scales. Shore A is for softer materials like rubber, while Shore B is used for very soft compounds. Shore C measures rubber-like materials that are slightly harder. Shore D measures harder plastics and harder rubbers. Shore M is for thin materials, measuring softer substances in specialized applications. Shore O is designed for very soft elastomers and similar materials. Each scale serves distinct industrial and scientific purposes.</p></p>
