<p><ul><li>Swan Neck Splints</li><li>Boutonniere Splints</li><li>Oval-8 Finger Splints</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ring splints market includes specialized products designed for finger joint stabilization. Swan neck splints are used to correct hyperextension of the proximal interphalangeal joint, ideal for conditions like rheumatoid arthritis. Boutonniere splints assist in realigning the finger in cases of flexion deformities, ensuring proper healing. Oval-8 finger splints, known for their versatility, support various finger injuries and deformities by restricting movement while allowing some flexibility. Together, these splints cater to diverse needs in rehabilitation and injury management.</p></p>
