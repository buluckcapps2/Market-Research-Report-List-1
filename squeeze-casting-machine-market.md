<p><p>Squeeze casting machines are categorized into two main types: hot chamber and cold chamber. Hot chamber squeeze casting machines utilize molten metal directly from a furnace, allowing for rapid production and efficiency, typically used for non-ferrous metals. In contrast, cold chamber squeeze casting machines require the metal to be preheated and poured into the chamber before compression, making them suitable for high melting point materials. Both types are essential for producing defect-free, high-strength components in various industries like automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
