<p><p>The concrete fasteners market is primarily divided into two main types: mechanical fasteners and adhesive fasteners. Mechanical fasteners include anchors, bolting systems, and screws designed to create a secure hold in concrete through physical means. In contrast, adhesive fasteners utilize specialized adhesives to bond materials to concrete surfaces, offering a non-intrusive fastening option. Both types are essential for various construction applications, providing strength and stability while addressing different installation needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
