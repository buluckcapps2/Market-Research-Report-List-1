<p><strong>The Smart Natural Gas Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AMR</li><li>AMI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Natural Gas Meter market is primarily divided into two types: Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR systems enable utility companies to gather consumption data remotely, using handheld devices or drive-by technologies, enhancing operational efficiency. In contrast, AMI provides a more integrated approach, incorporating two-way communication between meters and utilities, enabling real-time monitoring, dynamic pricing, and improved customer engagement. Both types aim to optimize energy management and streamline billing processes.</p></p>
