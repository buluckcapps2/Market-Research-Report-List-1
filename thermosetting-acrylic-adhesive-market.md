<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermosetting acrylic adhesive market is categorized into two primary types: water-based and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based adhesives utilize water as the main solvent, resulting in lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and making them more environmentally friendly. They are often preferred for indoor applications. In contrast, solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, offering stronger bonding and quicker curing times, making them suitable for demanding industrial applications. Both types cater to different requirements based on performance, environmental impact, and application methods.</p></p>
