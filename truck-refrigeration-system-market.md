<p><strong>The Truck Refrigeration System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split System</li><li>Roof Mount System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck refrigeration system market primarily features two main types: split systems and roof mount systems. Split systems utilize separate evaporators and condensers, allowing for improved efficiency and flexibility in installation. They are often preferred for larger trucks with varying temperature needs. Roof mount systems, on the other hand, are compact units installed on the truck's roof, optimizing space and reducing the risk of damage. These systems are popular for smaller vehicles, providing reliable cooling without compromising cargo space.</p></p>
