<p><p>The urethral stricture market is categorized into two main types: Anterior Urethral Stricture (AUS) and Posterior Urethral Stricture (PUS). AUS typically occurs in the penile and bulbar regions and is often caused by trauma or infection, affecting male patients more frequently. PUS, on the other hand, occurs in the prostatic and membranous regions, often resulting from surgery, injury, or inflammatory diseases. Understanding these types helps tailor treatment options and improve patient outcomes in urethral stricture management.</p></p>
