<p><p>The digital video content market consists of three primary types: SVOD (Subscription Video on Demand), AVOD (Advertising Video on Demand), and TVOD (Transaction Video on Demand). SVOD provides unlimited access to a library of content for a recurring subscription fee. AVOD offers free content supported by advertisements, attracting viewers with no upfront cost. TVOD allows users to purchase or rent individual titles, offering flexibility for those who prefer selective viewing without a subscription commitment. Each model caters to different consumer preferences and behaviors.</p></p>
