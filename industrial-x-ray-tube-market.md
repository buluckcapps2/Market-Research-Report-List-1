<p><strong>The Industrial X-ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary X-ray Tube</li><li>Rotating Anode X-ray Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial X-ray Tube market comprises two main types: stationary X-ray tubes and rotating anode X-ray tubes. Stationary X-ray tubes feature a fixed anode, making them suitable for applications requiring lower power and simpler design. In contrast, rotating anode X-ray tubes have an anode that spins during operation, allowing for higher power output and improved heat dissipation. This enables faster imaging and increased efficiency, making them ideal for demanding applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
