<p><p>The WiFi Smart Light Switch market comprises two main types: in-wall switches and stick-on switches. In-wall switches are permanently installed by replacing traditional light switches, offering a seamless integration into existing electrical systems and a clean aesthetic. Stick-on switches, on the other hand, are designed for easy adhesion to walls or surfaces, providing flexible placement without complex installation. Both types enable remote control and automation of lighting, catering to various user preferences and home configurations for convenience and energy efficiency.</p></p>
